Leftover Pizza

Got some leftover Fatte's 2 for 1 pizza? Well you've got yourself a few easy meals, and only for the price of 1!

Ingredients

  • Some pizza you bought a few days ago
  • Microwave

Instructions

  1. Grab as many slices as you would like
  2. Put the slices into the microwave
  3. Set the timer for 2 minutes
  4. Once the time is up, feel the pizza. If it's not to your liking, then add another minute
  5. Repeat step 4 until pizza is at the desired temperature
  6. Lunch time!
