Cereal

Start your day off right with a hearty bowl of cereal!

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 250 ml of cereal of choice
  • 125 ml of milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour the cereal into the bowl
  2. Pour the milk into the bowl
  3. Eat up
  4. Be a respectful roomate and wash your dishes as soon as you use them :)
