Popcorn

Another late night of studying? Popcorn is very filling, and can be a nice subsitute for dinner if you don't want to eat a whole meal. Just make sure to treat yourself to actual dinner tomorrow please ;).

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of popcorn
  • Microwave

Instructions

  1. Take the popcorn back out of the plastic wrapper
  2. Put the bag into the microwave
  3. Set the microwave timer to 4 minutes
  4. Listen to the popcorn kernels, they should slowly start popping and while increasing the popping speed. Once the popping slows down after that, you can stop the microwave
  5. Take the bag out of the microwave and enjoy
