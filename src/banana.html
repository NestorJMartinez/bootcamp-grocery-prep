Banana

So you're late to class again? Don't fret, a simple banana can carry you until your next break.

Ingredients

  • Banana

Instructions

  1. Grab the banana
  2. Peel the banana from the bottom to avoid the stringy thingys (or peel from the top if you want to)
  3. Make sure to throw that banana away when you're done, or even better, compost it
