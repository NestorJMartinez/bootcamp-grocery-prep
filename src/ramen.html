Instant Ramen

A nice switch up after having popcorn for the past two nights.

Ingredients

  • Cup of instant ramen
  • Pot
  • Fire

Instructions

  1. Pour some water into a pot
  2. Put the pot over a fire, and wait until the water starts to boil
  3. Pour the boiling water into the cup of noodles, and cover the lid
  4. Wait 2 minutes
  5. Uncover the lid, and dig in (watch out! the water is VERY hot)
