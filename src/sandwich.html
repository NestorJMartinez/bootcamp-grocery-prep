Sandwich

The classic American lunch meal. This is highly customizable so make sure to add your own personal twist to this food.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of bread
  • Mayo
  • Meat (or meat subsititute if you're vegan)
  • Lettuce
  • Anything you'd think would taste good

Instructions

  1. Spread the mayo onto the bread
  2. Add the meat onto one piece of the bread
  3. Add the lettuce, and any extra ingredients you added to the other piece of the bread
  4. Put one half of the sandwich onto the other, and voila, your sandwich is made
